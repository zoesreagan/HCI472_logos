Magpie Logos

Magpie is a fledgling 'no code/low code' project I am hoping to get off the ground in 2021. I selected the Magpie as the project's logo to represent ingenuiety, resilience and working smarter, not harder.

Magpies are a member of the Raven family of birds. Highly intelligent, Magpies are the only known species of bird to recognize their own reflection.

Inspired by the work of American artist Charley Harper, the logo itself is a formal exploration of the bird's side profile, with emphasis given to the high contrast plumage and the linear forms of the extra long tail feathers. The exaggerated tail 'lines' also create a 'ledger' for the project's title, which is using Futura.
